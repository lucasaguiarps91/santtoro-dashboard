How We Win
The Playbook
▶
Builder Outreach Sequence — 5 Steps
1
The Cold Call (Day 1)

Call the builder directly. Introduce Santtoro by name, mention the rebrand, ask: "What kind of projects are you working on this year?" Listen first. Then: "We significantly expanded our lines — Delano European, custom Cape factory, contemporary Florida (faster lead times), FABA wood. Worth 20 minutes at our SoWa showroom?"

2
Email Follow-Up (Day 2)

Send a clean one-page product brief: product lines, lead times, photo of the SoWa showroom. Include a direct calendar link to book a showroom visit. Subject: "Santtoro — cabinet partner for [Builder Name] projects."

3
Showroom Visit (Week 2–3)

Get them to 500 Harrison Ave. This is the conversion event. Walk through all product lines, show actual samples, discuss lead times and pricing tiers. Have Juliana (and owner when relevant) present. Bring specific product recommendations for their known project types.

4
Project-Specific Proposal (Week 3–4)

Ask: "What's one project coming up in the next 60–90 days where we could submit a quote?" Submit a clean proposal within 48 hours of the ask. Price competitively — winning the first project is more important than margin on project #1.

5
Close + Deposit (Week 4–6)

Explain payment terms clearly: 25% at signing, 50% when materials ordered, 25% at delivery. This is non-negotiable — cash is tight. Once the first project closes, maintain relationship with regular check-ins and invite to quarterly builder events.
